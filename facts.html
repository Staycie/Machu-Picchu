          Facts About Machu Picchu 

                1. Each stone was precisely cut to fit together so tightly that no mortar was needed to keep the walls standing
                
                2. Machu Picchu sits at 2,430 metres above sea level
                
                3. Machu Picchu is a Wonder of the World and a World Heritage-listed site
                
                4. The Inca empire was one of the largest in pre-Columbian America covering Argentina, Bolivia, Chile and Ecuador
                
                5. Llamas really *are* trying to photo bomb your holiday pics!
                
                6. There’s more than one hiking option 
                
                7. In the Quechua language, Machu Picchu translates to ‘Old Mountain’ or ‘Old Peak’
                
                8. A certain artist called Leonardo da Vinci painted the Mona Lisa (1503-1506) during the height of the Inca empire
                
                9. The natural setting of Machu Picchu is as impressive as the site itself
                
                10. Machu Picchu was used as an astronomical observatory
                
                11. It is not exactly known why the Incas abandoned Machu Picchu
                
                12. You can get your passport stamped at the entrance
